My country has a pretty flag with green and white colors and was designed by Sir Akinkunmi Taiwo after independence. The white color on the flag represents peace ;while the green color represents agriculture

The currency spent in Nigeria is naira and kobo which was introduced in January 1st 1973.Though the currency has lost its value and even wants to be changed to E-naira.

Nigeria is headed by a president President Muhammadu Buhari of the All Progressive Country(APC)assisted by his vice Professor Yemi Osinbajo.

She also has a beautiful logo called "coat of arms"which has different symbols on it and each represents a meaning.

  1. The two horses representing dignity.
  2. The eagle representing strenth.
  3. The silver Y representing confluence(the place where two rivers meet.
  4. The black shield representing the fertile land present in Nigeria.
  5. Then the motto of Nigeria stating:Unity and Faith,Peace and Progress.
